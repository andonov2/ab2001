AGRO-BEL 2001

About Us

Founded in 2001, our agricultural company has grown into a leading producer with more than 10,000 hectares of fertile arable land under careful, sustainable management. Thanks to an advanced irrigation network that services over 1,800 hectares, we achieve consistently high yields of our core field crops—corn, wheat, barley, and sunflower—while safeguarding soil health and water resources.

Driven by continuous innovation, five years ago we diversified into open-field vegetable production. Today, premium onions and potatoes complement our grains, allowing us to serve a broader market and make fuller use of our agronomic expertise. From precision planting and climate-smart irrigation to responsible stewardship of the land, every stage of our operation reflects our commitment to quality, sustainability, and long-term food security.
