ABOUT US

We are a leading animal feed factory in Bulgaria, established in 2015 with a vision to provide nutritious high quality products for livestock and poultry. To achieve that, we source our ingredients locally and have a long-standing relationship with a network of Bulgarian producers, which allows us to supply the best quality ingredients at competitive prices.

Furthermore, our modern factory equipment and the extensive experience of our experts allow us to provide a wide range of possibilities for your custom recipes. Through the feedback of our clients we observe that we stand out with our valuable cycle of recommendations and support throughout all aspects our relationship for the benefit of their farms.

Cereal Trade and Exports

We also trade and export different cereals (grains) such as wheat, corn, barley, oats, and soybeans. We have a strong network of suppliers and customers in the domestic and international markets and are committed to delivering our products on time and at competitive prices.
